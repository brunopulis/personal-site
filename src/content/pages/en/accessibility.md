## What am I doing?

To make the website accessible, I used WCAG 2.1. These guidelines explain how to make and keep web content more accessible to everyone and how to maintain its simplicity of use.

The guidelines have three levels of accessibility (A, AA, and AAA). I chose **level AA** for my website.

I chose this level because it is an intermediate level and covers several aspects of web accessibility. However, this choice does not invalidate the use of some AAA level success criteria.
