Get updates on the latest posts using your preferred method!
My content is available via _RSS_, _Microformats_, e-mail, _Bluesky_ e _Fediverso_.

## Subscribe with a feed reader

This blog supports subscriptions via RSS. Using a feed reader, you can subscribe to the RSS feed. Learn more about RSS at aboutfeeds.com.

You can subscribe to several feeds that I have organized, which are:

- [Blog Feed](/rss.xml)
- [Short Notes Feed](/rss-notas.xml)
- [My Favorite Movies Feed](/rss-filmes.xml)
- [My Blogroll Feed](/rss-blogroll.xml)
- [My Library Feed](/rss-biblioteca.xml)

## Subscription by email

The easiest way to subscribe is by email (https://brunopulis.substack.com/). Emails are sent once a week, and you can unsubscribe at any time.

## Follow me on Bluesky

If you use [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/), you can follow me at [@brunopulis.com](https://bsky.app/profile/brunopulis.com).
All posts on this site will be federated to the network via Brid.gy, so they will appear on the official identifier.

## Follow me on Mastodon or other fediverse networks

If you use a federated service such as [Mastodon](https://joinmastodon.org/), you can follow me by searching for <code>@bruno@brunopulis.com</code> on your server.
All posts on this site will be federated to the network (via ActivityPub).
Enter your account below to be redirected to your server's follow page.
